Privacy Policy

Introduction

At PulsePre (pulsepre.com), we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Personal data you provide: name, email address, or other contact details if you sign up for newsletters, comment, or contact us directly.
  • Automatically collected data: IP address, device type, browser, location (approximate), and browsing activity on our site via cookies and analytics tools.
  • Cookies and tracking: We use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences, serve relevant ads, and measure site performance. You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings.

How We Use Your Information

  • Deliver news, updates, and newsletters you subscribe to.
  • Personalize content and ads (through partners like Google AdSense, Adsterra, or affiliate networks).
  • Improve our website performance, usability, and security.
  • Respond to inquiries, feedback, and user support requests.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Service providers and partners: who help us operate the website (e.g., hosting, analytics, ad networks).
  • Legal authorities: if required to comply with laws or valid legal requests.
  • Business transfers: If PulsePre merges, is acquired, or undergoes restructuring, data may be transferred to the new entity.

Advertising & Affiliate Disclosure

  • PulsePre displays ads from networks such as Google AdSense and Adsterra. These providers may use cookies or device identifiers to serve relevant ads.
  • We may include affiliate links in our stories. If you purchase through those links, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights over your data, including:

  • Access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Opt-out of marketing emails by using the unsubscribe link.
  • Control cookies through your browser or device settings.

For EU/EEA and UK users, PulsePre follows the GDPR requirements. For California residents, you may have additional rights under CCPA.

Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, or as required by law.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-party Links

PulsePre may link to other websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices or content.

Children’s Privacy

PulsePre does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU). If we become aware that such data has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
